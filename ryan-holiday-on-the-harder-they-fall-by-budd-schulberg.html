  1. One of my favorite boxing books this book 'The Harder They Fall by Bud Schulberg. Now I've read this book at least two other times. I remembered this book as being a deeply influential life-changing book for me because it's the fictional memoir basically of a public relations man, a press agent as they used to call him, who's sort of in this corrupt broken world and he's struggling to get out of it but he likes the money, he likes the attention. He keeps telling himself he's going to get out of it, he just needs like one more job.
